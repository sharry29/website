Eight // Fifty-One // Twenty-Two

I visualized the numbers 8, 51, and 22 a different way each day for about two months. This was an attempt to practice ideation and to get more fluent in using D3 as a creative tool. I chose to limit myself in several dimensions (consistent data, limited color palette) so that I could focus on variety in others. One surprising benefit of this exercise was that it required me to accept that some of my ideas would be unsuccessful, and I enjoyed learning how to just move on.
